about me

Originally, I am from the south of Germany but I am currently residing in London, UK, studying Digital Media at the Metropolitan University and doing an internship as an Front-end Developer at SportPursuit. With my 24 years of age, I am currently on a quest for new challenges in this great environment. Over the past 5 years I have been working for several companies and in January 2010, I joined squadhouse-media, a small but powerful online media company, as a student-trainee. During this time I had the great opportunity to work on multiple exciting projects, which enabled me to increase my coding and design skills. I love the application of theoretical knowledge into practical work, while always focusing on state of the art ways to achieve customer satisfaction.
